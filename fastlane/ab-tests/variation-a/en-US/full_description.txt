Banjen is the simplest banjo tuner you'll ever use. Four buttons — one for each string. Tap a button, hear the note, tune your string to match. That's it.

No confusing needles. No flickering displays. No sign-up. No tutorial to swipe through. No email required. Just you, your banjo, and the right notes.

HOW IT WORKS

Open the app and you'll see four big, friendly buttons — one for each string of your 4-string banjo. Tap a button and it plays a clear, looping tone. Hold your phone near your banjo, pluck your string, and turn the tuning peg until the sounds match. When they match, you're in tune.

That's the whole app. Four buttons, four strings, done.

YOUR STRINGS

Banjen plays standard DGBD tuning for 4-string banjo:

• String 4 (thickest) — D
• String 3 — G
• String 2 — B
• String 1 (thinnest) — D

WHY TUNE BY EAR?

Here's something most tuner apps won't tell you: the best banjo players tune by ear. They always have. A good ear is the most valuable tool a musician can develop, and every time you match your string to a tone, your ear gets a little better.

You won't need this app forever — and that's the whole point. Every time you tune, you're training your ear to hear when your banjo sounds right. One day you'll just know.

SIMPLER THAN A CLIP-ON TUNER
