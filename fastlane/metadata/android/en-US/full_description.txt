Banjen is the simplest way to tune your banjo by ear. Tap a string button and hear a clear, looping reference tone. Match your banjo string to the tone, and you're in tune.

Perfect for 4-string banjo players using standard DGBD tuning:
• String 1 (thinnest) — D
• String 2 — B
• String 3 — G
• String 4 (thickest) — D

No complicated displays. No confusing needles. Just pure reference tones played through your speaker or headphones.

Designed for beginners who prefer ear-training over visual tuners. Simply tap, listen, and tune.
