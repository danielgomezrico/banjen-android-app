# Brand Positioning

## Unique Value Proposition

For Brazilian popular music beginners who distrust cold foreign apps, this is the ear-training tuner that sounds and feels like belonging — because it teaches you to hear the right note, not read a needle.

## Positioning Statement

For [cavaquinho, bandolim, banjo, and tenor guitar players in Brazil], [brand] is the [tuner app] that [develops your musical ear instead of replacing it] because [it plays the reference tone you match by feel, the way music is passed on in a roda de samba].

## Key Differentiators

**Functional:** Sine-wave reference tone you match by ear; no needle required; optional mic confirmation (not replacement)

**Emotional:** Feels like a musician friend tuning with you, not an instrument-checking machine

**Symbolic:** Rooted in Brazilian popular music culture — the roda, the shared tone, the oral musical tradition

**Cultural:** Portuguese-first, Brazilian-music-first, beginner-affirming

## Brand Feeling

Warm. Calm. Musical. Like hearing the right note for the first time and knowing it without being told.
