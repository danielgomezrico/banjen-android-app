# Market Audit — Ear-Training Tuner (Brazil)

## Competitive Landscape

| App | Positioning | Tone | Audience | Gap |
|-----|-------------|------|----------|-----|
| GuitarTuna | Global market leader, visual needle, gamified | Cold, corporate | Guitar-first, English-first | No Brazilian cultural resonance; no ear-training |
| Cleartune | Professional, chromatic needle, paid | Clinical, cold | Studio musicians | No Portuguese, no warmth, high price barrier |
| DaTuner | Free, utilitarian, ad-heavy | Functional, ugly | Budget-conscious players | Zero emotion; no ear-training; terrible UX |
| PitchLab | Technical, oscilloscope-style | Technical, masculine | Engineers and hobbyists | Overwhelming complexity; hostile to beginners |
| Boss Tuner | Guitar-centric, brand authority | Gear-head, professional | Guitar players who own Boss gear | Guitar brand lock-in; no cavaquinho identity |
| Afinador Pro (various clones) | Generic, keyword-stuffed | None — pure utility | ASO-driven downloads | No differentiation; needle-only |

## White Space

**No app in the Brazilian market speaks to the ear.** Every competitor owns the visual needle paradigm. The ear-training approach (play a reference tone, match by ear) is a genuine unclaimed territory.

**No app addresses Brazilian popular music identity.** Cavaquinho, bandolim, choro, pagode, samba are absent from every competitor's positioning, metadata, and UX. The instrument category that drives amateur music participation in Brazil is invisible to app stores.

**No app is warm and beginner-affirming in Portuguese.** Lúcia (cavaquinho beginner, roda de samba, Portuguese-first) finds every option foreign-feeling. That rejection happens at the discovery surface before she ever touches the product.

## Market Trends

- Android penetration in Brazil exceeds iOS; free-tier with ad revenue is the viable model
- "Cavaquinho" and "afinador de cavaquinho" are high-volume, low-competition ASO keywords in pt-BR
- Samba/pagode/choro communities are growing on YouTube and TikTok Brazil — organic acquisition surfaces exist
- Ear-training as a methodology is gaining traction in Brazilian music education YouTube (zero app product cross-sells exist in this content)

## Opportunity Summary

Own the position: **the tuner that teaches your ear, built for the sound of Brazil**. This is differentiated at every level — methodology (ear vs. needle), culture (Brazil vs. global), warmth (community vs. clinical). No competitor can claim this without abandoning their existing positioning.
