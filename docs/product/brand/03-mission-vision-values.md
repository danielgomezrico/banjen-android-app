# Mission, Vision & Values

## Mission

We help Brazilian popular music beginners find their note by ear — the way the music has always been passed on — so anyone can sit in a roda without fear of being out of tune.

## Vision

A world where tuning your instrument feels like joining a conversation, not passing a test.

## Core Values

**1. Ouvido primeiro (Ear first)**
In practice: every product decision prioritizes developing the user's ear over replacing it. The tone plays; the needle is secondary.

**2. Simplicidade que respeita (Simplicity that respects)**
In practice: we remove features that patronize. No gamification, no streaks, no badges. Harold and Lúcia are not children; they are musicians becoming.

**3. Som brasileiro (Brazilian sound)**
In practice: instrument categories, language, and cultural cues default to Brazilian popular music. Cavaquinho, bandolim, choro, pagode are first-class citizens — not afterthoughts.

**4. Calma que convida (Calm that invites)**
In practice: no layout shifts, no audio interruptions, no intrusive ads on the tuning surface. The moment of tuning is sacred and unhurried.
